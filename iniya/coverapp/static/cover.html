About the Book

This book tells the story of INIYA S, a determined "YOUNG ATHLETE" whose boundless energy on the field is matched only by the endless thoughts racing in her mind. She carries within her a spark — a flame of passion and imagination that drives her to push harder, dream bigger, and never give up.

"She runs faster than her doubts, dreams bigger than her fears, and proves that even an overthinking mind can fuel unstoppable strength."

INIYA S
She is an engineering student at Saveetha Engineering College who loves to participate in sports activity and as a AIDS student, she enjoys to explore new technology.
